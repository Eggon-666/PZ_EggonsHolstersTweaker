title=Eggon's Holsters Tweaker
description=This mod allows to tweak holsters' weght reduction. By default it is set to 75%, but it can be changed by the player by modifying mod's files (instruction shortly).
description=
description=[b]Side effect[/b]
description=As the game does not allow modifying the weight reduction of attachments in form of parametrization, the mod performs a bit of a hack that results in incorrect weight "when not equipped" shown in the tooltip when an item is in holster.
description=
description=[b]Existing saves[/b]
description=The mod should be compatible with existing saves. Unattaching and attaching an item might be required in order to "kick in".
description=
description=[b]Compatibility[/b]
description=The mod should work on all vanilla and custom items that provide (holsters) or use (pistols, etc) attachment slot of type 'holster'.
description=
description=[b]Requirements[/b]
description=This mod requires my utility mod Eggon's Modding Utils to function.
